Special Order

1. Figure out what the special order is for. Take interest in the customers life and reason for the order.
2. Navigate to order management system and click new order. Choose the candy types and quantities that you can from our catalog. For candy types that aren't in the catalog, there will be a special request text box at the bottom of the order management system.
3. Gather the customers payment information. If there were candy requests inform the customer that special requests can take up to a month to complete.
